# Mobile Quickstart

## What you do on your phone

1. Open the GitHub app or `github.com` in your phone browser.
2. Sign in to the account that owns this repo.
3. Open this repository.
4. Tap `Actions`.
5. Tap `Phone Trigger Self Hosted`.
6. Tap `Run workflow`.
7. Pick the action you want:
   - `open-codex`
   - `status-check`
8. Tap `Run workflow` again.

## What happens next

- The workflow runs on your self-hosted runner.
- The desktop action should happen immediately if the runner is online.

## What you do not need

- You do not need to type code on the phone.
- You do not need to open a terminal on the phone.
